Our Vision

Computing, Data Science and Society at Berkeley

Image

Our lives and our society are being changed by computing, statistics, and data science: the platforms on which we innovate, communicate, transact, make decisions, and allocate resources. At Berkeley, we believe computing and data science are for everyone. We need diverse perspectives, experiences, and expertise to ensure research and applications represent the needs and interests of society. 

The College of Computing, Data Science, and Society (CDSS) is the first new college at UC Berkeley in more than 50 years. Our vision is to:

Image

 

Educate all students to approach data ethically and masterfully

Image

 

Advance the state-of-the-art in the core of computing, data science, and statistics

Image

 

Found new fields bringing computing and data science together with other disciplines

Image

 

Make sustained progress on issues of human and societal significance

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, Belonging, and Justice

Diversity, equity, inclusion, belonging, and justice are essential elements of educating a rising generation of students, building a collaborative presence across campus, and serving society. 

Our Pathway to Becoming a College

Berkeley’s impact in computing and data science research and education is transforming the field. The College of Computing, Data Science, and Society (CDSS) collaborates with partners across campus and beyond to develop inclusive educational pathways and innovative data-based solutions to real-world problems.
