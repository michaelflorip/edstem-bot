Anthony Suen
Data Science Discovery Program Director
Data Science Undergraduate Studies
Discovery Program
Anthony is the Director of the Data Science Discovery Program. He was the first staff member recruited to be part of the Data Science Initiative in 2015 and founded many data science programs and initiatives such as: Data Science Discovery, Data Science Modules, Data Scholars, Data Peer Consulting, National Workshop on Data Science Education, Data Science Student Teams, Global Adoption, Connector Assistants, Online Learning Services, Infrastructure Team (creator of Ottergrader), UC Berkeley Cloud Meetup, Data Science Nexus, and Data Collaboratives.
Anthony was previously a Data Science Fellow at the Berkeley Institute for Data Science, Schenider Fellow at the Natural Resources Defense Council's Beijing Office, and co-founder of the nonprofit Energyfolks. Anthony holds a Masters in International Policy from Stanford University and a Masters of Information Management and Systems from UC Berkeley. At Berkeley, Anthony founded the first Data Science club on campus and taught the Lean Startup course alongside Steve Blank at the Haas School of Business.
