Advising at Data Science Undergraduate Studies
Data Science Advisors are available to help undergraduate students navigate UC Berkeley, understand requirements, choose classes, and make the most of your college experience.
Center for Student Affairs, Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences
The EECS Center for Student Affairs provides academic and administrative services to current and prospective undergraduate and graduate students to achieve their academic and career goals.
Student Support at Department of Statistics
The Department of Statistics provides support for prospective students and current students in the undergraduate and graduate programs. They are available for academic and personal advising and encourage students to meet with them to discuss academic plans, coursework, personal concerns, and any other matters.

Undergraduate Degree Requirements
Students who were admitted to Berkeley prior to Fall 2024 have the option of completing their CDSS degrees by meeting the L&S requirements or the CDSS course requirements, but CDSS policies will apply. Beginning Fall 2024 and onward, newly admitted students must meet CDSS course requirements and policies.

Explore our Departments and Programs
The College of Computing, Data Science, and Society (CDSS) at UC Berkeley brings together programs, schools, departments, and partners to create accessible and equitable educational opportunities and catalyze groundbreaking research that meets society's greatest challenges. We are reimagining inclusive collaboration to tackle the technical, scientific, social, and human dimensions of urgent challenges in health and biomedicine, climate and sustainability, and human welfare and social justice.
