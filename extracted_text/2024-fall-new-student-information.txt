Welcome to Data Science at UC Berkeley!
Admission to Data Science major is handled by the UC Berkeley Admissions office. For questions related to eligibility or likelihood of admission, contact UC Berkeley Admissions.
We’re excited to tell you about our program and help you navigate the major. Please explore the events and resources on this page to learn more about Data Science at Cal!
