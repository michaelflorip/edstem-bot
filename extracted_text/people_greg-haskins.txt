Greg Haskins
Executive Assistant to the Associate Provost and Dean
College of Computing, Data Science, and Society
Greg Haskins has worked at West Coast University and Mount St. Mary’s University prior to coming to UC Berkeley as an Executive Assistant.  He has had a long list of administrative roles at other companies such as Estee Lauder Corporation in New York City, and Disney Channel, Staples Center, and the Demand Media offices in Los Angeles.  His well-rounded background also includes 18 years teaching college medical workshops through Health Education Advocates as well as 11 years as an entertainer at Walt Disney in Orlando and Japan.  He looks forward to working with all the staff and students at UC Berkeley.

ghaskins@berkeley.edu
