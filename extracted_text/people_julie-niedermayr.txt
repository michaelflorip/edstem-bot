Julie Niedermayr
Academic and Staff Human Resources Manager
College of Computing, Data Science, and Society
Julie is a longtime veteran of Berkeley academic personnel and staff human resources policy and protocol with extensive knowledge regarding academic recruiting, retention, threshold and merit reviews, and business process improvement.  During her time at Berkeley, Julie has worked in units including the Department of Plant and Microbial Biology, the College of Environmental Design, Biosciences Divisional Services, and the School of Public Health. Julie lives in Oakland with her family and enjoys working in her garden, cooking, and having fun with her cat and three dogs.
