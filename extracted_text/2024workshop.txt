5 day format for 2024
Location: Berkeley, California
Dates: Jun 24-28, 2024
Monday - Tuesday, June 24-25 - California Learning Lab Data Science Grand Challenge grantees. (invitation only)
Wednesday - June 26 - UC Berkeley Data 8 and Program Overview
Thursday - Friday - June 27-28 - Panels of Thematic Interest on Data Science Education
Registration
The workshop is free for attendees.
