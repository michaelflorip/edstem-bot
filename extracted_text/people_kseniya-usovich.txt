Kseniya Usovich
Project Manager, Online And Outreach Programs,
Data Science Undergraduate Studies
Kseniya is passionate about making Data Science Education accessible.  She is currently working to expand DSUS online offerings as a project manager with DSUS collaboration with RTL and EdX.  Kseniya works extensively to manage the DSUS outreach efforts and create community through California Alliance for Data Science Education. She has worked for several years with Cal NERDS to lead workshops. She was also a community college transfer to UC Berkeley.
