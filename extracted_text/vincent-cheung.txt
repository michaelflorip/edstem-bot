Vincent Cheung '03, UC Berkeley Foundation Trustee
CEO, Nan Fung Group
Vincent Cheung is chief executive ofﬁcer of the Nan Fung Group, responsible for strategic development as well as overall operations. He is also the founder and chairman of Nan Fung Life Sciences, a global life sciences investment platform. Prior to joining Nan Fung Group in 2009, Cheung had extensive experiences in the ﬁnancial sector. Cheung is also the founder and chairman of Hong Kong Life Sciences Society, a youth organization aiming to build a dynamic life sciences ecosystem in Hong Kong with a focus on nurturing young talents. He is also a court member of the University of Hong Kong, serves as a trustee for the UC Berkeley Foundation, and is a member of the UC Berkeley Chancellor's Asia Council. Cheung graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with honors in molecular and cell biology.
