Patty Anderson
Office Manager
College of Computing, Data Science, and Society
Patty Anderson is the office manager for the College of Computing, Data Science, and Society, where she works on a broad range of administrative and organizational efforts. Patty is a proud first-generation college student. She received her master’s degree in human resources and organizational development from the University of San Francisco and her bachelor’s degree in psychology from California State University, Chico. She started her career in human resources. She later worked as a change management consultant for PriceWaterhouseCoopers. Most recently, she worked as an office manager for the Orinda Union School District. Patty and her husband Rob are enjoying an empty nest as their two children attend college in southern California. In her free time, you can find Patty in her garden, cooking or on a hike with her dog.
