Jacob Gallice
Executive Director, Educational Programs and New Initiatives
College of Computing, Data Science, and Society
Jacob Gallice brings over a decade of experience across higher education, consulting, and financial services. He is a firm believer in the power of education to serve as a driver for change. Prior to joining the College of Computing, Data Science, and Society (CDSS) in January 2024, Jacob worked as Executive Director for the Master of Financial Engineering program at the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley. During his time at Haas, Jacob oversaw academic experience, admissions, career services, and employer relations, as well as strategic planning. Before joining Berkeley, Jacob worked at the NYU Stern School of Business to develop recruitment and marketing strategies for the school’s full-time Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs. He was also active in the launch of the university’s one-year focused MBA program in technology. Prior to entering the field of higher education, Jacob worked at the global leadership consulting firm Heidrick & Struggles and investment bank Goldman Sachs. Jacob received his MBA from the NYU Stern School of Business and bachelor’s degree from Binghamton University, graduating summa cum laude.
