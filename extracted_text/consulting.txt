Discovery Consulting
Launched in Fall 2017, the Data Science Discovery Consultants (formerly Data Peer Consultants) are a team to provide technical help to the students and partners engaged in discovery with anything data-related. We strive to make data science accessible across the broader campus community.

Discovery Consultants Team
The Data Science Discovery Consultants team is made up of top UC Berkeley undergraduate students, rigorously trained in research pedagogy, data analysis, and machine learning. Their expertise, coupled with our program's resources, delivers cutting-edge solutions that drive real-world impact. Our commitment to driving social impact and promoting collaboration across multiple disciplines is reflected in the exceptional quality of our team.

Our Services
Discovery & Frontdesk Consulting
If you are a Discovery Student or Partner, schedule an appointment above and we will answer any questions about Discovery logistics, any data science and coding-related needs (debugging, project scoping, coding doubts, etc.), as well as providing you with free Cloud Computing Credits (Savio, Microsoft Azure).
If you are Berkeley faculty, staff, postdocs, graduate and undergraduate students at all levels of expertise, feel free to schedule an appointment with D-Lab Frontdesk.
Discovery Project Management
Collaborative projects are a fundamental part of your academic journey, and the ability to work effectively with project partners and teams is a valuable skill for both your studies and future career. See our memo about project management here, which will provide you with a high-level overview of best practices to consider when engaging with your project partner, forming and working with your team, and developing a project plan and timeline.
Discovery Workshop
Our Consultants hold training workshops on relevant topics throughout the semester. The workshops are determined by Discovery student needs and will cover topics like Machine Learning, web scraping, and data visualization.
Cloud Computing
For Discovery Students & Partners, we offer free Cloud Computing Credits. Schedule an appointment with our Discovery Consultants above to learn more and get access to these credits!
