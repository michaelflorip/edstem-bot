College of Computing, Data Science, and Society (CDSS) Advisory Board
Qi Lu
Founder and CEO, MiraclePlus
Qi Lu is founder and CEO of MiraclePlus, a fast growing startup accelerator for tech-driven founders. His previous roles include Partner at Y Combinator & Head of YC Research; President and Chief Operating Officer at Baidu; President of Online Service Division & Executive Vice-President of the Application and Services Group at Microsoft; various roles at Yahoo from Engineer to Executive Vice President; and Research Staff Member at IBM Almaden Research Center. Lu received his Ph.D. in computer science from Carnegie Mellon University.
