California Alliance for Data Science Education (CADSE)
There is an urgent need to expand access, diversify participation, and support data science teaching across higher education institutions in the state of California.

College Systems
Timeline
University of California
Several UCs have large undergraduate Data Science offerings, several offer Data Science as a specialization within Statistics or Computing, and several are currently building programs
California State University
There is a range of how CSUs are deploying Data Science.  Cal Poly SLO has built an in depth minor and Cal Poly Humbodlt is introducing a new major in 2023. CSU Fresno has a new interdisciplinary minor and CSU East Bay has built an alternative pathway without calculus.
California Community College
CCCs are at working on adapting existing course frameworks to Data Science instruction, as well as building new certificate, transfer pathways, and building linkages across departments
