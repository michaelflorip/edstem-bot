Travis Caskey
CDSS HR Analyst
College of Computing, Data Science, and Society
Travis is returning to UC Berkeley after graduating with his bachelor's degree in 2016. In his role as the CDSS HR Analyst, he will support and advise CDSS staff and leadership on a broad range of human resources activities. Prior to joining the college, he worked at a Berkeley-affiliated non-profit, the International Computer Science Institute (ICSI). At ICSI he was responsible for a variety of administrative and HR functions.
