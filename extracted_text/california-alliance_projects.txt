California Alliance for Data Science Education (CADSE)
There is an urgent need to expand access, diversify participation, and support data science teaching across higher education institutions in the state of California.

Working groups: Funding Sources for Data Science Initiatives, Standard Requirements for DS Curriculum, Transfer Model Curriculum, Associates Degree for Transfer in DS
Tracking new data science offerings at various campuses
NSF HDR Grants
Central Coast Data Science Partnership - Cal Poly, UCSB, California State University, San Bernardino (CSUSB) and SBCC
Data Science Training and Practices - UC Irvine, California State University at Fullerton, and Cypress College
Data Science Career Pathways in the Inland Empire - UC Riverside,  CSU San Bernardino, Riverside Community College
University of California
Mapping majors and proposed majors in the UC system
Pilot Infrastructure Collaboration
Working with 2i2c and NSF Cloudbank to pilot the provision of Juptyerhubs for new instructors at community colleges.
California Learning Lab

California Community Colleges
Capacity building for instructors-  Developing community of practice workshops among community colleges.
Creating new pathways- Organizing regular update meetings where CCCs can present and answer questions on DS transfer pathways they’ve built, crafting guidelines.
Supporting course articulation- Creating resources on standardized requirements for a course to articulate. Connecting faculty with policymakers like UCOP and CSUCO.
