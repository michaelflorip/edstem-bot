Meet with a Data Science Advisor
Data Science Advisors are available to help undergraduate students navigate UC Berkeley, understand requirements, choose classes, and make the most of your college experience. Book an appointment or come to our virtual drop-in advising hours!
Human Contexts and Ethics
The HCE Program brings together faculty and students to teach core courses for the Data Science major and minor (Data 4AC and Data C104) and develop curriculum packages for technical classes at Berkeley and beyond. See our curriculum page to learn more about our work and to access our public materials.
Student Opportunities
Looking for data science research programs, internships, and other academic enrichment options? Data Science Undergraduate Studies offers a wealth of resources available campus-wide to support learning, research, and discovery.
Data Science Student Teams
Students interested in developing their technical and analytical skills, gaining professional experience, and making a difference in the data science community at Cal are encouraged to apply!
Data Science Discovery Program
The Data Science Discovery Program incubates and accelerates data science research by connecting UC Berkeley students to high impact academic, government, non-profit, and industry projects across the globe. We're currently welcoming UC Berkeley students and partners to join us!
Data Scholars
The Data Scholars Program provides spaces where students from many disciplines and backgrounds learn together, develop their data science skills, and co-create a diverse community.
